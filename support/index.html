RubberScore Support

RubberScore is a companion app for social bridge sessions. It is designed for quick score entry. It helps groups track score, review hands, and wrap up with a clear recap.

Home Privacy Policy

Modes of Play

RubberScore supports two ways to play.

  • Rubber — traditional rubber bridge scoring
  • One Session — casual, continuous play with no rubber prompts, just keep playing

FAQ

How do I change player names?
More → Quick access → Edit Seat Names.
How do I change partners?
More → Quick access → Change Partners.
Where is hand history?
Tap “View all hands” below the score to expand or collapse history.
How do I export a summary?
More → Session Details → Export.
Does the app sync or upload data?
No. All data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it.

Privacy

RubberScore stores all session data locally on your device. No data is uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to export and send it.
