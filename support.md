# RubberScore Support

RubberScore is a companion app for rubber bridge sessions.  
It helps groups track score, review hands, and wrap up with a clear recap.

## Contact

If you have questions, suggestions, or encounter an issue, please contact:

**Email:** 

RubberScore@jfergus.com 

## Privacy

RubberScore stores all session data locally on your device.  
No data is uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to export and send it.

You can read the full privacy policy here:

https://jtfergus.github.io/rubberscore/privacy.html
