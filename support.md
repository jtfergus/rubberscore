# RubberScore Support

RubberScore is a companion app for social brige sessions.

It is designed for quick score entry.
It helps groups track score, review hands, and will wrap up with a clear recap.

## Modes of Play

RubberScore supports two ways to play. 
- Use **Rubber** for traditional rubber bridge scoring.  
- Use **One Session** for casual, continuous play with no rubber prompts—just keep playing.

## FAQ

**How do I change player names?**  
More → Quick access → Edit Seat Names.

**How do I change partners?**  
More → Quick access → Change Partners.

**Where is hand history?**  
Tap “View all hands” below the score to expand or collapse history.

**How do I export a summary?**  
More → Session Details → Export.

**Does the app sync or upload data?**  
No. All data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it.
